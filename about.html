About Me

Data Scientist and Investment Professional with a passion for math, markets, & real data. Over a decade experience in multi-asset trading at Tiger Global and Morgan Stanley, specializing in equity derivatives & FX. Skilled at data analytics, machine learning, and statistical modeling & analysis, including:

Data Analysis - Python, R, Numpy, Pandas, SciPy, StatsModels

Big Data - AWS, Hadoop, Hive, Spark (and PySpark), SQL (PostgresSQL and SQLAlchemy)

Machine Learning (supervised & unsupervised) - Scikit-Learn, Clustering, Classification, Regression (Linear & Logistic), Decision Trees/Random Forest, Boosted Trees etc.

Deep Learning - Keras, TensorFlow, Pytorch, Convoluted Neural Networks (CNNs)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) - NLTK, TextBlob, Word2Vec, Topic Modeling, Sentiment Analysis

Data Visualization - Tableau, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Webscraping - Beautiful Soup, Selenium, MongoDB, Scrapy, lxml

Web Apps & Design - Flask, dash, plotly, D3.js, HTML, CSS, Jekyll, LaTeX, Google Analytics

Nearly 20 years experience as an educator focused on math & science, educational-tech solutions, and volunteering in underserved communities.

Currently seeking data science opportunities at firms that employ technology and data analytics towards trading, investment strategies, and research. I am also interested in educational and social activism projects.

Feel free to contact me at clarence@cognosis.solutions
