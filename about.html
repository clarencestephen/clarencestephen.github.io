About Me

Data Scientist and Investment Professional with a background in finance and education. I have 10 years experience in multi-asset trading, having led equity derivative & FX strategies at Tiger Global after trading at Morgan Stanley. On tech side, my experience includes automation, analytic tool development, project management, and data analytics leveraging python, SQL, VBA, machine learning, FactSet, Bloomberg, & CapIQ. I have nearly 20 years experience as an educator focused on math & science, educational-tech solutions, and volunteering in underserved communities in New York. I am currently seeking data scientist opportunities at firms that employ technology and data analytics towards trading, investment strategies, and research. I am also interested in educational and social activism projects.
